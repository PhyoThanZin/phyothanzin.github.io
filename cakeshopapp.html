Cake Shop App – UI/UX Case Study

A delightful and modern mobile app designed to make ordering cakes as simple as scrolling through your favorite treats. This project showcases the full design process from user research to prototyping and final mockups.

UI/UX Thinking Process

1. Problem Definition

People often struggle to find reliable, visually appealing cake delivery options that feel premium yet accessible. The goal was to design an app that brings a joyful browsing and ordering experience with a smooth, intuitive flow.

2. Wireframing

Low-fidelity sketches were created to plan user journeys such as browsing cakes, adding to cart, and checking out. The focus was clarity and reducing steps per task.

3. Color Choices

The Cake Delivery App embraces a vibrant and cheerful color palette designed to evoke joy and appetite. Primary accents like #DC1A4A give energy to calls-to-action, while soft backgrounds such as #FCE8ED and #FAFAFA provide a light, welcoming environment for browsing.

4. Font

By sticking to one typeface, Manrope, we maintain a clean, modern, and cohesive look throughout the app. Weight variations (regular, medium, bold) create hierarchy and readability without the need for additional fonts.

5. Components & System

Buttons, cards, modals, and inputs were built consistently for scalability. This allowed for a cohesive UI with reusable elements across the app.
